Projects

My projects have primarily been part of a the VGDev club at Georgia Tech. Here are some of the games I have worked on, and the roles I held in them.

Willow is my one of the first two projects I have worked on. It is a VGDev club project led by Yoon Lee and Mitchell Phillip. It is a 2D bullet-hell rougelike, featuring a weapon crafting system and a gameplay loop that focusing around recruiting NPCs to a growing festival. My role for this project was minor design work, including weapons, rooms, enemies, NPCs, and items.

Starfall is the other of my first two projects. It is also a VGDev club project, led by Ben Lock. It is a 3D rougelike built around fast movement and extreme aggression. I was the head designer on this project, focusing on creating and tweaking core combat and mechanical systems in the game, such as the "Link" system that the combat is based around.
